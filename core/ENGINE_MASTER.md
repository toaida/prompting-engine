## V16 ANTI-REPETITION HARD RULES

Before generating any 10-image set, verify:

1. **No repeated silhouette** across 10 consecutive images
2. **3+ different camera distances** per 10 images
3. **2+ different outfit families** per 10 images
4. **Minimum 3 "not-a-photo" moments** per 10 images
5. **Object continuity maintained** — recurring objects appear
6. **Beauty state continuity** — degradation carries forward
7. **≤2 hero shots** in any 10-image set
8. **≥1 ugly-cute / failed beauty** moment per 10 images
9. **Environmental independence** — world feels alive without her
10. **≥1 social interruption / contamination** per 10 images
