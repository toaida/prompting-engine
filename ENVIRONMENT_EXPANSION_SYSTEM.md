## WHAT THIS SYSTEM IS

V14.5 environments were becoming repetitive — rooftop, café, beach, pool, generic nightlife.

V14.6 replaces "pretty background" with **human-world behavioral ecosystems**:
environment is not backdrop, it's the causal system.

80+ environment combinations achievable from 5 new families × specific behaviors.

---

## PHILOSOPHY

**"The image should feel like a moment inside a real human world.**
**Not scenery-with-person. Environment→Physical causation→Body response→Camera intercept.**
