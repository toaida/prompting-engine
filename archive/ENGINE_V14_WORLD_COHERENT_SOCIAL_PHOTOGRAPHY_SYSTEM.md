# 5. WORLD RULE ENGINE

The most important V14 upgrade.

Do not start with fragments like:

- girl
- outfit
- pose
- lighting

Start with a single coherent WORLD RULE.

A world rule defines:

- place
- time
- air
- temperature
- humidity
- lighting sources
- camera type
- social situation
- emotional pressure

Examples:

## Humid convenience-store walk home

“Humid Hong Kong summer midnight, 80% humidity, convenience-store fluorescent spill mixing with apartment TV blue light, sticky air after walking home, smartphone night mode struggling in low light.”

## Rain-after-MTR exit

“Rain just stopped outside an MTR exit, wet pavement reflecting green traffic lights and shop signs, humid air still hanging, phone camera HDR clipping the reflections.”

## Old mall afternoon

“Old Hong Kong shopping mall afternoon, beige-green fluorescent lighting, low ceiling, mirrored pillars, cold air-con against humid outdoor skin, casual friend-shot while waiting.”

## Beach café heat

“Late-afternoon beach café after swimming, warm sea haze, wet towels on plastic chairs, melting shaved ice, sun bouncing from sand and water, smartphone HDR slightly overexposed.”

The world rule should govern:

- hair behavior
- skin sheen
- fabric drape
- facial expression
- camera flaws
- color tone
- body posture
- background residue

### Worm's eye

⭐ V14.6 CRITICAL DISTINCTION:

❌ WRONG — Worm-eye AS glamour: Camera below subject, looking up at body, emphasizing curves/bust/legs. Subject is hero. Environment is backdrop. [VIOLATION]

✅ CORRECT — Worm-eye AS environmental immersion: Camera at ankle-knee height, looking at world from below. Emphasizing scale of environment, smallness of human within it, dominance of atmospheric conditions. Subject is PART of the world. Environment is the hero. [V14.6 PERMITTED]

Moderation check: "Is the environment dominant? Is the human small? Is this about atmospheric presence, not body display?"
Full system: see `WORM_EYE_ENVIRONMENT_SYSTEM.md`
