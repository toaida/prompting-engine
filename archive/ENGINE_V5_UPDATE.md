# lil.troublr Hybrid Engine V5
## Social Realism / Camera Sociology / Human Behavior Upgrade

V5 evolves the project from:
- image prompting
into:
- human-behavior simulation

Core V5 upgrades:
- CAMERA_OPERATOR_IDENTITY_SYSTEM
- SOCIAL_PHOTO_LOGIC_SYSTEM
- HUMAN_CAMERA_ERROR_SIGNATURE_SYSTEM
- MICRO_ASYMMETRY_EXPRESSION_SYSTEM
- HUMIDITY_HAIR_BEHAVIOR_SYSTEM
- LOCAL_TEXTURE_DENSITY_SYSTEM
- POSTURE_REVEAL_MAPPING_SYSTEM
- CONTEXTUAL_SUBJECT_SCALE_SYSTEM
- ENVIRONMENTAL_STORYTELLING_SYSTEM
- MAINTENANCE_REALISM_SYSTEM

Core philosophy:
The strongest realism comes from:
- believable timing
- social causality
- emotional reachability
- imperfect observation
- environmental authenticity
- camera intentionality

NOT:
- glamour exaggeration
- explicit exposure
- perfect posing

The camera is now treated as:
a social participant.

Every image must imply:
- who took the photo
- why the photo exists
- what emotional relationship exists between viewer and subject

Highest-value anti-AI markers:
1. flyaway hair
2. asymmetric expressions
3. imperfect framing
4. slight camera failure
5. environmental clutter
6. posture instability
7. realistic fatigue
8. local texture density
9. transitional expressions
10. believable social timing

V5 transition direction:
V6 should focus on:
- motion realism
- smartphone artifact realism
- temporal realism
- group social dynamics
- hand/finger realism
- interrupted attention realism
