# AMATEUR SNAPSHOT SYSTEM

## Core Principle
Human snapshots feel half-caught, not fully completed.

## Disposable Camera Logic
- harsh direct flash
- dim environment
- imperfect framing
- accidental tilt
- casual timing
- uneven exposure

## Anti-AI Realism
Do not optimize for perfection.
Optimize for:
- interrupted moments
- emotional timing
- awkward crops
- imperfect autofocus
- social spontaneity
