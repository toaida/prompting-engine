# 1. Core Philosophy

V14 shifts from:

girl exists in scene

to:

scene creates girl behavior.

The image must be driven by:

- world rule
- environmental physics
- camera operator
- social reason
- emotional timing
- posture mechanics
- fabric causality
- narrative residue
- human imperfection
- attraction as emergent realism

---

# 9. Posture / Fabric Reactions

Use causality:

activity -> posture -> support -> fabric response

Examples:

- seated twist creates diagonal folds
- leaning forward bunches sleeves
- bag strap pulls fabric
- humidity softens shirt drape
- car seat shapes posture
- towel compresses under fingers
- skirt hem shifts from turning
- cardigan slides from lowered shoulder
- sleeve wrinkles from phone-holding arm

Avoid direct anatomy-first wording.

---

# 10. Anti-AI Asymmetry Markers

Use:

- asymmetrical smile
- cheek lift unevenness
- gaze reconnecting late
- one eyebrow slightly higher
- hair not evenly styled
- mixed light on face
- non-perfect crop
- environmental clutter
- local texture
- real skin pores
- fabric seam shadows
- slight fatigue

AI often removes what humans recognize as life.
