## MODERATION RULES (8 HARD RULES)

1. **No glamour framing** — worm-eye = environmental immersion, NEVER = body elongation
2. **Photo existence required** — every image needs WHY it exists
3. **Fabric physics required** — explain WHY clothing looks this way
4. **Proof-of-life objects required** — 3-5 imperfect objects visible
5. **Spatial depth required** — always foreground + subject + background
6. **Camera awareness Level 3/4 for intimate content** — no Level 1 with exposure
7. **Gravure = soft focus skin, sharp eyes** — never clinical/harsh
8. **Anti-fetish physics** — wet fabric = weather-caused, garment physics = realistic
