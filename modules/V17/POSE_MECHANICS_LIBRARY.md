## PHILOSOPHY

**Core Problem:** AI-generated images show "poses" as static frozen positions — no understanding of how the human body gets there. The result is mannequins that look posed, not humans caught mid-behavior.

**The Fix:** Describe the MECHANICS — how weight shifts, how joints rotate, how momentum carries through. Poses become BECOME-positions, not static poses.

## POSE MECHANICS BY TYPE

### 1. SITTING ON EDGE

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Approach surface
2. One hand reaches back to feel for edge
3. Knee bends, body lowers
4. Weight sinks into sit-bone
5. Feet plant for balance
6. Torso settles, spine curves
```

**Pose Descriptions (use these, not "sitting pose"):**
- "Lowered onto edge, one hand still reaching back"
- "Weight sinking into mattress, spine still straight"
- "Just felt edge, knees bent, about to sit"
- "Settled into seat, lower back against chair"

**Avoid:** "sitting elegantly" / "model sits on chair"

---

### 2. LYING ON STOMACH

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Knees touch surface first
2. Hands plant for support
3. Hips shift forward over knees
4. Torso lowers, elbows bend
5. Chin comes to rest on folded arms
6. Pelvis relaxes, weight drops
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Knees on towel, arms folded under chin, pelvis dropped"
- "Upper body lowered onto forearms, hip still slightly raised"
- "Relaxed onto stomach, cheek on pillow, one knee bent"
- "Getting comfortable on towel, arms adjusting"

---

### 3. LEANING ON SURFACE

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. One leg steps back, foot plants
2. Hip shifts back toward surface
3. Upper body leans, shoulder touches wall/object
4. Arm reaches to brace or rest
5. Head tilts toward surface
6. Weight distributes through lean
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Hip shifting back to wall, shoulder making contact"
- "Leg stepped back for balance, upper body tipping into rail"
- "One arm braced on railing, weight tipping forward"
- "Shoulder against window, head following toward glass"

---

### 4. WALKING / MOVING

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Back foot pushes off ground
2. Weight transfers to front foot
3. Hip rotates forward
4. Opposite arm swings forward
5. Head leads slightly into direction
6. Momentum carries body through frame
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Mid-step, back foot pushing off, front foot landing"
- "Momentum carrying forward, arm swinging opposite to leg"
- "Walking toward camera, hip rotation visible"
- "Stepping off curb, slight bob, weight not yet settled"

---

### 5. REACHING FOR SOMETHING

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Eyes find target
2. Shoulder retracts, arm lifts
3. Hand extends toward object
4. Body leans in direction of reach
5. Opposite arm counterbalances
6. Fingers approach, adjustment micro-movements
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Shoulder lifting, arm reaching toward phone on table"
- "Body leaning forward, hand closing distance to glass"
- "Arm extended, fingers adjusting angle to grab cup"
- "Reaching up to shelf, opposite hand steadying hip"

---

### 6. TURNING TO LOOK

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Eyes move first, find target
2. Head follows, neck rotates
3. Shoulder on opposite side lifts slightly
4. Upper back rotates
5. Lower body stays or minimal rotation
6. Result: three-quarter view
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Head mid-turn, neck still rotating, body still forward"
- "Shoulder lifting as head turns back over shoulder"
- "Half-turned, pelvis still facing original direction"
- "Over-the-shoulder look, neck tension visible"

---

### 7. LYING ON BACK

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Back of knees touches surface
2. Body lowers backward
3. Head follows, pillow receives
4. Arms settle to sides or overhead
5. Pelvis releases, spine settles
6. Breath exhale, shoulders drop
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Back lowering toward mattress, knees still bent"
- "Head touching pillow, arms settling to sides"
- "Knees up, back still arched, not yet relaxed"
- "Weight sinking into surface, breath half-out"

---

### 8. SQUATTING

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Feet shoulder-width or wider
2. Knees bend, hips push back
3. Torso leans slightly forward for balance
4. Arms extend for balance
5. Weight drops into heels
6. Lower back maintains neutral curve
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Knees bent, hips dropping, heel contact firm"
- "Squatting at beach towel edge, about to sit fully"
- "Heels planted, torso leaning forward for balance"
- "Low squat, knees out, weight in heels"

---

### 9. CLIMBING / STEPPING

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. Foot lifts, toes point up
2. Shin lifts toward knee
3. Thigh raises, knee leads
4. Foot reaches for surface
5. Weight transfers, standing leg activates
6. Back leg follows through
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Foot on step, weight shifting forward, back leg straight"
- "Mid-step onto curb, shin vertical, heel lifting"
- "Climbing out of pool, hands gripping edge, leg pushing up"
- "Stepping over threshold, toe contact first, heel follows"

---

### 10. LYING ON SIDE

**Mechanics Sequence:**
```
1. One shoulder drops toward surface
2. Upper arm follows, head settles
3. Lower body lowers, hip then knee
4. Body curls into fetal-adjacent
5. Top knee bends forward
6. Spine curves, weight on hip point
```

**Pose Descriptions:**
- "Lowered onto side, hip taking weight, knee following"
- "Side position, top knee bent forward, head on pillow"
- "Curled on side, spine curved, shoulder relaxed"
- "Turning to side, arm still under head"

## POSE MECHANICS INTEGRATION RULES

### Rule 1: Describe the Becoming, Not the Been

```
BAD: "Subject sitting on bed"
GOOD: "Weight lowering onto bed edge, hand reaching back to feel mattress"
```

### Rule 2: Include One Micro-Movement

Every pose should have one element of in-progress movement:
- "Knee about to bend"
- "Hand not yet landed"
- "Weight not yet fully transferred"
- "Breath half-inhaled"

### Rule 3: Connect to the Surface

Real poses involve body responding to surface:
- Hand reaching for chair
- Feet planted for sitting
- Shoulder against wall
- Back against headboard

### Rule 4: Chain the Sequence

Describe 2-3 steps in the chain:
```
"Foot lifted, shin raising, knee leading toward surface, body lowering, weight not yet settled"
```

### Rule 5: Avoid Static Pose Keywords

Replace these:
- ❌ "posed" → ✅ "weight settling"
- ❌ "standing" → ✅ "standing on one leg, other foot shifting"
- ❌ "lying down" → ✅ "back lowering, pillow receiving head"
- ❌ "leaning" → ✅ "hip shifting back toward wall"
